    Spotify Setup Wizard

    This wizard stores your Spotify app settings locally for browser sign-in.

    1. Open Spotify Developer Dashboard. If you are not signed in, you may be redirected to developer.spotify.com. Log in from the top-right corner, then click your profile (top-right) and go to Dashboard.
    2. Click Create app and fill in:
      • App name: Spotify Manager (or any name you prefer)
      • App description: Advanced Spotify playlist management desktop app
      • Website: http://localhost
      • Redirect URIs: add the exact URI shown here: http://127.0.0.1:3000/api/auth/spotify/callback
      • Which API/SDKs are you planning to use?: Check the Web API checkbox
      • Terms of Service: Check the Terms of Service checkbox
    3. After creating the app, open it and copy your Client ID.
    4. Paste it below and click Test & Save.
